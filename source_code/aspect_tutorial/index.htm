Aspect Ratio

Choosing the right aspect ratio of a chart is very important. The aspect ratio is simply the length and width ratio of a chart and can change the appearance of a trend drastically. Using a suboptimal or problematic aspect ratio can lead to misleading charts. However, aspect ratios are subjective, as there are multiple trends to be seen. For example, take a look at the chart taken from https://eagereyes.org/basics/banking-45-degrees . The data is exactly the same, but the aspect ratios and trends appear very different. Aspect Ratio is not a design constraint when the axes are logarithmic.
